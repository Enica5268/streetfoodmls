Asam Laksa Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 500 gram mackerel fish (or canned sardines)
  • 500 gram thick rice noodles
  • 10 cups water
  • 3 stalks lemongrass
  • 5 pieces asam gelugur
  • 50 gram tamarind paste
  • 1 tablespoon shrimp paste
  • 3 pieces dried bunga kencong
  • sugar and salt
  • 100 gram red chilies
  • 150 gram shallots
  • 25 gram garlic and galangal
  • 1 cucumber, ginger flower, purple onion and lime
  • 1 bunch of fresh mint leaves


Instructions:

  1. Ground all the spices and herbs and add a bit of water to help it going to prepare for ground spices.
  2. Bring 10 cups of water to boil and lower the heat to medium low to let the mackerel fish simmer for 10 minutes.

  3. After that, seperate the fish meat and bone. Save the bones and head.

  4. Then, add the ground spices and herbs, fish bones and the rest of the ingredients for soup base.

  5. Bring it to a boil and then lower the heat, cover the pot with a lid and let it simmer for 30 minutes. Then, strain the stock and discard all the solids. Stir the flaked fish meat back into the soup and season it.

  6. Finally, served the noodles, fish and soup into a bowl. Top it with sliced cucumber, sliced onion, mint leaves and finely chopped ginger flower plus squeezing some lime juice.
