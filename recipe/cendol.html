Cendol Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 10 pieces pandan leaves
  • 500 ml water
  • mung bean flour
  • corn starch
  • 2/4 tablespoon salt and sugar
  • 600 ml coconut milk
  • coconut sugar or gula Melaka
  • 1 tablespoon akaline water (optional)
  • redbeans (optional)
  • shaved ice


Instructions:

  1. Wash and cut the pandan leaves and place it in a blender with water and blend till the leaves are all very fine and strain it to get pandan juice. Mix all the flour, sugar, alkaline water (if using), and pandan juice mixture in a saucepan.

  2. Continue to stir until it is really thickened and turns into translucent. Stir until smooth and no lumps.

  3. After 10 to 12 minutes, transfer the jellylike cendol into strainer or colander with large holes and use the spoon to press through the holes.

  4. Put the cendol into cold ice water and let it solidify.

  5. Put cendol and coconut milk together with gula melaka or coconut sugar. You may add red beans if you want. Congrats, a lovely dish has done.
