Nasi Lemak Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups coconut milk
  • 2 cups water
  • 2 cups rice (rinsed and drained)
  • 1 (1/2 inch) piece fresh ginger, peeled and thinly sliced
  • 1 whole bay leaf
  • 1 onion
  • 3 shallots
  • 3 clove garlics
  • ¼ cup tamarind juice (or substitute with lemon juice)
  • 3 tablespoons white sugar
  • salt to taste
  • oil
  • garnish (raw peanuts, boiled eggs, cucumber, anchovies)
  • Sambal (spicy chili paste)


Instructions:

  1. Make the rice and cook it with coconut milk, water, fresh ginger, bay leaf and a pinch of salt.
  2. Cover and bring to a boil over medium heat. Reduce the heat and simmer until tender, 20 to 30 minutes. Discard bay leaf and keep rice warm until garnish and sauce are ready.

  3. While the rice is cooking, make the garnish: Heat the oil over medium-high heat. Stir in peanuts and cook briefly, until lightly browned. Remove peanuts and place onto paper towels to soak up excess grease.

  4. Then, stir in anchovies and cook, turning occasionally, until crisp, 2 to 3 minutes.

  5. Make the sauce: Stir in onion, shallots, and garlic, cook until fragrant, 1 to 2 minutes.

  6. Mix in chile paste and cook for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally, if mixture is too dry, add water as needed.Stir in anchovies and cook for 5 minutes. Then, stir in tamarind juice, sugar, and salt, simmer until sauce is thick, about 5 minutes.

  7. Lastly, put the rice into plate and place the sambal, topping with garnish prepare earlier. Congrats, a lovely dish has done.
