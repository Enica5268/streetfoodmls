Char Kway Teow Recipe

Ingredients:

  • flat rice noodles
  • large shrimp
  • fish balls
  • eggs
  • mungbean sprouts
  • garlic chives
  • dark soy sauce
  • regular soy sauce
  • oyster sauce
  • chinese sausages and blood cockles (optional)
  • salt and sugar
  • oil and garlic
  • banana leaves


Instructions:

  1. Preheat wok over very high heat and add cooking oil when you see smoke started to rise
  2. Add garlic, slices of Chinese sausage, fish cake, and shrimp, stir fry until the shrimp just turn pink and not fully cooked yet. Push them to the side of the wok

  3. Add noodles,sauce seasonings and eggs, stir fry until it coats the noodles.

  4. Turn off the heat and add beansprouts and garlic chives.

  5. Dish out from the wok and put it on a plate with banana leaves. Congratulations, a savory noodles has been done.
